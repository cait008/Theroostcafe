Non-Dairy Options

We offer oat milk, almond milk, soy milk, and coconut milk. Non-dairy alternatives often have a smaller carbon footprint compared to traditional dairy. However, consider the water usage of almond milk or the land impact of soy. Make the choice that aligns with your values!

Dairy Options

Our dairy products come from local farms that prioritize animal welfare. While dairy has a larger carbon footprint than non-dairy options, supporting sustainable and ethical farming can help reduce the impact.

Food Choices

From dairy-based treats like cream cheese bagels to non-dairy pastries, each choice has its ecological story. Dairy-based foods generally have a higher footprint due to emissions from livestock, while non-dairy alternatives might use more resources like water and land.
